## 2. Summary

Animal Kings is a board game. The App does not require you to create an account and we do not ask you to provide your name, email address, phone number, contacts, photos, camera, microphone, or precise location in order to play.

The current App includes Google Mobile Ads / AdMob on supported mobile platforms. Because ads are served by a third-party advertising provider, Google and its partners may process certain data from your device and your interactions with ads, as described below.

## 3. Data we process directly

We do not directly collect account registration data because the App has no account system.

The App may process gameplay information on your device, such as the current board state, moves, language selection, sound setting, and undo/reset actions. This information is used to run the game interface and is not sent to our servers by the App.

If you contact us for support, we may process the information you choose to send us, such as your email address, message content, device or app details you include, and any follow-up communications. We use this information to respond to your request, keep records of the support interaction, and protect our legal interests.

## 4. Advertising and third-party data processing

The App uses Google Mobile Ads / AdMob to display ads, including interstitial ads on supported mobile platforms.

When ads are requested, Google Mobile Ads may automatically collect and share data for purposes such as ad serving, ad measurement, analytics, fraud prevention, security, and improving ad performance. Depending on your platform, device settings, region, consent choices, and Google's technology, this may include:

- IP address, which may be used to estimate general location.
- Device identifiers, including advertising identifiers where available.
- App and ad interactions, such as app launches, taps, ad impressions, ad views, and video views.
- Diagnostic and performance information, such as crash, launch, hang, and energy usage data.
- Advertising data, such as ads shown in the App.

We do not provide Google with your name, email address, contacts, precise GPS location, camera data, microphone data, or account information from Animal Kings, because the App does not collect those categories directly.

Google may process data independently under its own privacy policies and advertising terms. For more information, see:

- Google Privacy Policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy
