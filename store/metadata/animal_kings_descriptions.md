## English

Short description:
Flip, move, and capture in a fast animal strategy board game.

Full description:
Animal Kings is a quick local strategy board game inspired by classic animal chess. Red and Blue face off on a compact 4 x 4 board where every match begins with hidden pieces and a fresh random layout.

Each turn gives you one clear choice: flip a hidden piece, or move one revealed piece one square up, down, left, or right. Higher-ranked animals capture lower-ranked animals, equal ranks eliminate each other, and the Rat has a special trick: it can defeat the Elephant.

Win by capturing every opposing piece or leaving your rival with no legal action. Built-in rules, sound effects, undo support, piece counters, and 30+ interface languages make Animal Kings easy to learn and quick to replay whenever you want a compact tactical challenge.
